T&S Connect Four

Instruction

Hello! My name is Tanya. My final project is a game called Connect Four.
The rules for Connect Four are simple:
Try to build a row of four checkers while keeping your opponent from doing the same.
Sounds, easy, but it's not! You must think in a whole new way to block your opponent's moves too!

How To Play

  1. Decide who plays first. Players will alternate turns after playing a checker.
  2. On your turn, click on of your checkers on the board ANY of the slots at the bottom.
  3. Play alternates until on player gets FOUR checkers of his or her color in a row.
    The four in a row can be horizontal, vertical or diagonal.
  4. If you're the first player to get four of your checkers in a row, you win the game!
